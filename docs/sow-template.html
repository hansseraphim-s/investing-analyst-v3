What we build

Production-grade algorithmic trading infrastructure for sophisticated investors, family offices, and small RIAs. The systems we deliver are backtest-first, risk-disciplined, and fully transparent: no opaque alpha boxes, no SaaS lock-in, no overnight promises. Every promotion from paper to live is gated by mechanical walk-forward validation against your strategy and your universe.

  • Broker integration — Alpaca and Interactive Brokers (paper + live), atomic bracket orders, options support
  • Market data — real chains + Greeks, earnings calendar, dynamic momentum scanner across ~4,500 tradeable US equities
  • Risk discipline — CVaR daily-loss breaker, portfolio Greeks limits, concentration caps, atomic stop/target brackets
  • Validation — walk-forward harness with documented promotion gates (no live trading without passing them)
  • Dashboard — auth-gated portal at your domain, real-time equity curve, vs-SPY benchmark, full trade journal
